Napkind Fabric About Share

The average garment gets worn seven times before it gets thrown out.* Be a part of the solution. Donate it if its still good enough to wear. If it’s ripped or stained there is still a chance to give it a second life. Hyperlocal donations accepted in Pennsylvania Chester/ Delaware/ Montgomery counties. Accepting woven cotton/cotton blend (like men’s dress shirts, and sheets) not knit cotton (like tshirts) which are too stretchy.
*Thomas, Dana, 1964- author. Fashionopolis : the Secrets Behind the Clothes We Wear. New York :Penguin Press, 2022.
